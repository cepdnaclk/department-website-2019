Optical Networks Research Group (ONRG)



Optical networking is one of the hot areas in computer-networking/telecommunications. The elegant idea of transmitting information optically through (optical) fibersand optically-switching them at intermediate switches has been considered to be a promising solution for handling the explosive growth of the Internet traffic. We, in the Optical Networks Research Group,are working on several dimensions of this area spanning backbone, metropolitan, and access parts of telecommunications networks to overcome several challenges and to realize the full potential of this elegant idea.

Our Current Research Interests

  • Elastic Optical Networks
  • Optical switch architectures for backbone and metro optical networks
  • Survivability in optical networks
  • WDM optical circuit switching networks
  • WDM optical burst switching networks
  • IP/MPLS-over-WDM Networks
