Manjula Sandirigama

B.Sc. Eng. (Peradeniya), PhD (UNSW)

Dr. Manjula Sandirigama received Ph.D. (2001) in Computer Science from the University of Ehime, Japan and M.Sc (1998) in Computer Science from the University of Ehime, Japan, and B.Sc. (1991) in Electrical Engineering from University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka. He was appointed a Senior Lecturer at University of Peradeniya in the Department of Computer Engineering. He is also an Attorney-at-Law, Sri Lanka. His primary expertise is in secure communication protocols. He also has an expertise in Intellectual Property Rights and teaches IP for general engineering students. He has authored a book on IP for science and technology students.
