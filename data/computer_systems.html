Computer Systems Engineering: Industrial Networks Research Group



Use of computer control systems in controlling every industrial system is becoming a standard. The technology is moving towards massively integrated industrial networks forming smart systems such as smart grids and smart cities etc. Introduction of massively distributes sensors in the form of sensor networks and with intelligent sensors in the form of Internet of Things (IoT) increase the complexity of these control systems and networks. This movement introduces several challenging problems for computer engineers. Therefore Computer Systems Engineering: Industrial Networks Research Group of the Department is conducting research to address the problems in the areas of automation, security and data mining in these massively integrated computer control systems.
