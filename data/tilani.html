Tilani Gunawardena

B.Sc. Eng. (Peradeniya), PhD (University of Basilicata)

Tilani Gunawardena received Ph.D.(2015) in Computer Science from the University of Basilicata,Italy and B.Sc Engineering (2010) degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka. She works as Temporary Lecturer at University of Peradeniya. Her research interests are GPU computing, Cloud Computing, Big Data , Data Mining and Machine Learning , Natural Language Processing, Neural Networks and Artificial Intelligence.
