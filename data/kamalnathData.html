Kamalanath Samarakoon

B.Sc. Eng. (Peradeniya), M.Eng. (AIT), Ph.D. (Cardiff), C.Eng. (SL), C.Eng. (UK), MIET (UK) , MIE (SL), MIEEE (USA)

Dr. Kamalanath Samarakoon received Ph.D. (2012) in Electrical Engineering from the Cardiff University, UK and M.Eng (1996) degree in Computer Science from the Asian Institute of Technology (AIT), Thailand, and B.Sc.Eng (1991) in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka. He was appointed a Senior Lecturer at University of Peradeniya in the Department of Computer Engineering in 2007. Prior to joining the University he has worked in Ceylon Electricity Board as a Chief Engineer (IT) and Electrical Engineer.  He is also the Director in Engineering Education Unit, at the Faculty of Engineering. He has co-authored more than 25 papers in international peer-reviewed journals and conferences on power system control and smart grid.  He is a Chartered Engineer of Sri Lanka and the UK and a Member IESL, IEEE and IET.
