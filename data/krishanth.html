Krishanthmohan Ratnam

BSc Eng (Peradeniya), PhD (NUS)

Dr.Krishanthmohan Ratnam received the B.Sc.Eng. degree in Computer Sciences from the University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka, in 2001, and the Ph.D. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the National University of Singapore (NUS) in 2008. After completing his Ph.D. degree, he held a research position in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, National University of Singapore till 2011. He joined the University of Peradeniya in 2011, where he is currently a Senior Lecturer in the Department of Computer Engineering. He has served on the technical program committee of international conferences and also as a reviewer for many journals and conferences of international repute. His research interests include WDM optical circuit and burst switching networks, IP/MPLS-over-WDM networks, and survivability issues. His research findings have resulted in many research papers published in reputable international journals and conferences. He received a National Research Council (NRC) Merit Award for Scientific Publication in November, 2015. Dr. Krishanthmohan is a member of IEEE.
