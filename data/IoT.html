IoT and SDN lab



What we do?

We perform cutting-edge research on Wireless IP core and access network part of the LTE architecture, with focus on

  • Fundamentals of secure mobile communication and effective data transfer
  • LTE-advanced and 5G
  • Cognitive communication techniques in the LTE domain
  • Cyber-physical systems and Internet of things
  • Software Defined core and wireless networks
  • OpenFlow based IoT hub for the implementation of micro services
  • Secure lightweight authentication and communication protocols.
  • Green communications
  • OpenFlow based virtualization and cloudification
